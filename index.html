Story of The Sqzit

The Story

In the late 1980s, General Mills introduced Squeezit, a fun, squeezable juice drink with colorful bottles and playful names like “Silly Billy Strawberry,” capturing kids' imaginations.

By the 1990s, Squeezit became a cultural icon with its portable design and vibrant flavors. Despite its popularity, it was discontinued in the early 2000s, yet remained beloved.

Nostalgia for Squeezit led to limited reissues, delighting original fans and introducing a new generation to its joy. Squeezit remains a symbol of 90s childhood fun.
