Who is the SQZIT? Here is the answer!

Who we are

In a world where beverages merely quenched thirst, Sqzit emerged with a bold vision: to redefine the drinking experience. Founded on principles of sustainability and interaction, Sqzit aims to go beyond refreshment, fostering meaningful connections with consumers.

With a steadfast commitment to sustainability, Sqzit implements eco-friendly practices throughout its operations, ensuring every sip is a step towards a greener future. But Sqzit is more than just a beverage brand—it's a platform for interactive engagement and shared experiences.

Through vibrant packaging and innovative marketing, Sqzit invites consumers to join in its mission of creating a brighter, more playful, and sustainable world. Together, we sip, we connect, and we celebrate the joy of Sqzit.

“Sqzit aims to revolutionize the beverage industry by offering a delightful and sustainable drinking experience. We strive to create a brand that goes beyond mere refreshment, fostering meaningful connections with our consumers through playful interactions and sustainable practices.”


- SQZIT Team 2024

Our Missions

Redefine the Drinking Experience

Sqzit aims to elevate the beverage industry by offering more than just refreshment. We strive to create memorable drinking experiences that foster meaningful connections with our consumers.

Champion Sustainability

At Sqzit, we are committed to sustainability in everything we do. From eco-friendly manufacturing processes to recyclable packaging, we ensure that every sip contributes to a greener future.

Foster Meaningful Connections

Sqzit serves as a platform for interactive engagement and shared experiences. Through vibrant packaging and innovative marketing, we invite consumers to join us in creating a brighter, more playful, and sustainable world.

Promote Consumer Engagement

We believe in actively engaging with our consumers to co-create experiences that resonate with them. By listening to their feedback and preferences, we continuously refine and improve our products and brand experience.
